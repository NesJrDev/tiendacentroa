APERITIVOS / APPETIZERS

Desayuno con Aguacate / Breakfast with Avocado

Huevos, frijoles, crema, queso, aguacate and 2 tortillas. / Eggs, cream, cheese, avocado, beans and 2 tortillas. Breakfast is offered all day every day.

Desayuno con (1) Pupusa Revuelta / Breakfast with (1) Mixed Pupusa

Plátano, frijoles, queso, crema, huevos y 2 tortillas. / Plantain, slices, beans, cheese, 2 egg and 2 tortillas. Breakfast is offered all day every day.

Desayuno con Casamiento / Breakfast with Rice and Beans

Plátano, frijoles, arroz, queso, crema, huevos y 2 tortillas. / Plantain, beans, rice, cheese, cream, egg and 2 tortillas. Breakfast is offered all day every day.

Huevos Rancheros

Frijoles, queso, crema, 2 huevos y 2 tortillas fritas. / Beans, cheese, cream, 2 eggs, 2 fried tortillas. Breakfast is offered all day every day.

Desayuno Sencillo / Simple Breakfast

Rebanadas de plátano, frijoles, queso y 2 huevos. El desayuno se ofrece todo el día, todos los días. / Plantain, slices, beans, cheese and 2 eggs. Breakfast is offered all day every day.

Desayuno a la Mexicana / Mexican Style Breakfast

Plátano, frijoles, queso, crema, huevos, 2 tortillas, chile dulce o jalapeño, cebolla y tomate. / Plantain, slices, beans, cheese, 2 eggs, bell peppers or jalapeño, onion and tomato. Breakfast is offered all day every day.

Desayuno con Mortadela / Breakfast with Bologna

Mortadela, huevo, vegetales, aguacate, arroz, frijoles, 2 tortillas y queso. / Bologna, egg, vegetable, avocado, rice, beans, 2 tortillas and cheese. Breakfast is offered all day every day.

Desayuno Mortadela Guisada / Fried Bologna Breakfast

Mortadela guisada, casamiento, queso, aguacate, huevos and 2 tortillas. / Fried bologna, rice and beans, cheese, avocado, eggs and 2 tortillas. Breakfast is offered all day every day.
